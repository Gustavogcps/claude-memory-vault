# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussion focused on the work. Harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language are not welcome. Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, and contributions that violate this, and may block repeat offenders.

Report concerns privately via the repository's Security/contact channels.
